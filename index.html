About Cosmo India

  • Cosmo India Pvt. Ltd has pioneered in the field of manufacturing wheel balancing weights. CIPL is a synonym of expertise and excellent customer satisfaction. We have established ourselves as one of the major market leaders with enviable reputation over the last 25 years. We are the largest wheel balancing weights manufacturer in India with a plant capacity of 25 million parts per annum. Apart from our quality excellence and competitive prices, the greatest advantage of CIPL has always been the short and promised timeframes of delivery. It is majorly for this reason, and our quality excellence & competitive prices, CIPL has added many eminent and renowned Global OEMs like Maruti Suzuki, Mahindra & Mahindra, Fiat, Wheels India Ltd etc. and aftermarket customers to its esteemed clientele.

Why Balancing?

01

"Tires endure constant wear and tear, serving as a crucial interface between your vehicle and the road. As such, wheels play a pivotal role in shaping your car's overall performance."

02

"Continued driving exacerbates weight imbalances, resulting in instability. This compromises the car's handling, posing a safty hazard by depriving it of a stable foundation."

03

"Wheel weights counterbalance tire imbalances, ensuring even weight distribution properly, and that is resulting in fuel savings, extended tire life, and enhanced safety and comfort."

04

"During rotation, centrifugal forces impact the center of vehicle's axle, necessitating precise wheel balancing. Despite advanced techniques, weight imbalances are inevitable."
