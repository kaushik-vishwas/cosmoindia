Products :

All of our product range comes with Cosmo Branding. However, private branding options are also available. Please contact us to discuss your requirements.

Steel (Fe) Wheel Balancing Weight

Our range of steel weights comes in Grey Coated and Zinc Electroplated variants to deliver corrosion resistant high-quality products. OE manufacturers prefer steel weights due to a lower carbon footprint, lower cost, and eco-friendly recycling. The whole range of Steel Weights is available in OE as well as budget options, ensuring three different price points for each product.

Adhesive Weights

From -20°C to up to 50°C, our wide range of adhesives is available for all weather conditions and budgets. Each of the following variants is available in strips ranging from 5 grams to 100 grams and in rolls of 5 Kgs. The whole range is 3.8 mm thick and comes with an easy liner for easy peel-off and application. Our range is suitable for Four-Wheeler and Two-Wheelers with widths in the range of 15mm up to 22mm.
